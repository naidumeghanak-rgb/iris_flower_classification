About Iris Flower Classification

This project uses Machine Learning to classify Iris flowers into three species based on sepal and petal measurements.

🌸 Setosa

Iris Setosa has small petals and wide sepals. It is the easiest species to identify and is clearly separable from others.

🌼 Versicolor

Iris Versicolor has medium-sized petals. It lies between Setosa and Virginica and is moderately difficult to classify.

🌺 Virginica

Iris Virginica has large petals and long sepals. It is the most complex species and sometimes overlaps with Versicolor.
